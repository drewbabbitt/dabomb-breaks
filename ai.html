Under the Hood

What the Engine Does

Live Comp Aggregation

Pulls recent sales from eBay, marketplace listings and live-show sales so every price reflects what the card actually sold for — not an asking price.

Price Alerts & Watchlists

Save any card and get pinged the moment it crosses a price threshold, so you never miss a buy window or a good time to sell.

Instant Consignment Quotes

Snap a photo or enter a card's details and get a comp-backed payout estimate in seconds — before you ever ship a box.

Inventory Valuation

The entire vault is marked-to-market nightly, so total value, gainers and coolers are always current — not stale spreadsheet math.

Ownership Ledger

Tracks which cards are consigned versus in-house, plus payout status for every consignor, in one transparent record.

Ad Automation

Feeds fresh listings straight into shopping and social ad campaigns, so newly priced inventory starts finding buyers automatically.
