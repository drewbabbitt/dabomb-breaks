Know Before You Go

Break Formats Explained

PYT

Pick Your Team

You choose exactly which team you're riding into the break. Costs a bit more than random, but you control your outcome — perfect for repping your squad or targeting a rookie class you're chasing.

RT

Random Team

Teams get assigned live by randomizer once every spot sells out. Cheaper buy-in, pure variance, and honestly the most fun way to watch — you find out what you've got right along with everyone else.

DZ

Divisions / Razz

Spots are grouped by division instead of single teams, so you're covering multiple squads per slot. Razz-style breaks add a draft or elimination twist on top — higher variance, bigger upside.

P

Personals

Buy an individual pack or slot and every card from it is yours alone — no sharing a box with the room. Great low-commitment way to try a new product or TCG set like Pokémon 151.

Good to Know

Break FAQ

What happens if my team hits nothing?

That's the nature of the hobby — variance is part of the fun. You'll still get every base card and insert pulled for your spot, and full box/case results get posted after every stream so you can see exactly what came out.

How is shipping handled?

Pulls are sorted, sleeved and toploadered within a day or two, then shipped tracked and insured — our average ship time is about 2 days from the break.

Can I combine shipping across breaks?

Yes. Win spots in more than one break the same week and we'll hold everything to ship together in one insured package — just message us on Whatnot.

Are spots refundable?

Spots lock in once claimed since the box or case is purchased and allocated live. If we cancel or reschedule a break, you're fully refunded or rolled into the next comparable one.
