Good to Know

Break & Marketplace FAQ

What is a card break?

A break is a live event where sealed boxes or cases are opened on stream. You buy a spot — a team or random slot — and every card pulled for your spot ships straight to you.

How does consignment work?

Send us your cards; we photograph, price and list them across the marketplace and our live shows. You keep ownership until the moment it sells, and you can track status anytime.

Are cards graded & authentic?

We carry PSA, BGS and SGC slabs plus verified raw singles. Every listing shows grade, latest comps, and a 12-month price history chart.

Where do you stream?

Live on Whatnot at whatnot.com/user/dabomb_breaks — 4.8★ rated, 459+ orders shipped, ~2-day average ship time.
