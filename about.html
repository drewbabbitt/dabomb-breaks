The Story

Built by Collectors,
For Collectors

DaBomb Breaks started the way most great hobby shops do — a camera, a stack of boxes, and a community willing to watch us rip live. Everything since has been about earning trust one shipped card at a time.

We started ripping live on Whatnot with nothing but a laptop, a light, and a genuine love for the hobby. No script, no hype machine — just honest breaks, fair pricing, and showing every card the moment it hit the table.

That approach built something real: a 4.8★ rated shop with 37 reviews, 459+ orders shipped, and an average ship time of about two days. Collectors kept coming back because they knew exactly what they were getting, every time.

Now we're building the full galleria — a marketplace for graded slabs and raw singles, a consignment service that keeps sellers in the loop from submission to payout, and an AI engine that watches the market so pricing is never a guess. Same values, bigger platform.
